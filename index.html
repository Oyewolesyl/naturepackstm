The Problem

Nigeria's packaging crisis has no middle ground.

01

Reliant on Plastic Packaging

Widely used plastic packaging tears easily, cannot handle weight or moisture, leading to significant product damage and business losses for MSMEs.

02

Environmental Pollution

Single-use plastics contribute 26% of total environmental pollution across Nigeria, creating lasting damage to ecosystems and communities.

03

Affordability vs Durability

Businesses face a difficult choice between affordable but weak packaging or durable but expensive options — with no middle ground. Until now.

The Material

Agricultural waste, turned into strength.

Banana and plantain fibers from agricultural residues create a fiber-rich material with exceptional strength and durability.

  • Stronger than low-grade plastic under weight and handling
  • Moisture-resistant natural fiber construction
  • Sourced from local agricultural communities
  • Fully biodegradable and circular economy aligned
