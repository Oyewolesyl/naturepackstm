The Problem

Nigerian businesses deserve better than what they have been given.

01

Plastic Packaging Fails

The plastic bags most businesses rely on tear under weight, fall apart in moisture, and damage products. Every spoiled delivery is money a small business cannot afford to lose.

02

It Is Killing Our Environment

Single-use plastics make up 26% of Nigeria's total environmental pollution. The bags that wrap our goods today are choking our land and water tomorrow.

03

No Good Option Exists

You either buy cheap packaging that destroys your goods, or expensive packaging that destroys your margins. Nobody has solved this for Nigerian MSMEs. Until now.

The Material

What farmers throw away is what we build with.

After a banana harvest, the stems get left to rot. We collect them, process the fibers, and use them to make packaging that is tougher than the plastic most businesses are stuck with.

  • Stronger than standard plastic under weight and rough handling
  • Holds up against moisture without losing its shape
  • Sourced directly from local farming communities
  • Breaks down naturally when it has done its job

How It Works

From a farm in Nigeria to your customer's hands.

01

Collect the Stems

We gather banana stem waste from local farms and communities before it gets discarded.

Local SourcingZero Waste
02

Break It Down

The stems go through a pulping process that extracts and cleans the natural fibers.

Fiber ExtractionNatural Process
03

Form the Sheets

Sheet PressingQuality Check
