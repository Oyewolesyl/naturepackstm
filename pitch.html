The Packaging Problem

Plastic waste is piling up while useful farm material is being thrown away.

Businesses still need packaging every day. The issue is what happens after that packaging is used. Too much of it ends up in gutters, waterways and open spaces, while banana stems with useful fibre are left behind after harvest.

01

Plastic Pollution

Millions of plastic bags and packaging materials are discarded every year, contributing to pollution in our communities, waterways, and ecosystems across Nigeria.

02

Wasted Agricultural Resource

Large quantities of banana stems are discarded after harvest, even though they contain natural fibres that can be turned into useful material.

03

An Untapped Opportunity

That gap gives us a clear opening: take material that farms do not use and turn it into packaging businesses actually need.

Why This Matters to Us

Waste should not be where the story ends.

At Looped Innovation, we saw two problems sitting side by side. Businesses needed better packaging, and farmers had banana stems they could not use after harvest. NaturePacks™ connects those dots.

The work matters because it can reduce waste, support local production and give small businesses packaging they can feel proud to hand to a customer.

Introducing NaturePacks™

From banana stem waste to packaging people can use.

NaturePacks™ turns discarded banana stems into strong natural fibre sheets. Those sheets are then cut, finished and formed into bags, boxes and custom packaging for growing businesses.
